Questions

Good to know

What's the actual difference between the tiers?+
Free gives you your own dashboard to track Measurements and Body Composition on your own. Community Membership adds our WhatsApp community, member-only Library content, and a skin pack discount — still no clinical involvement. Starter and Longevity Membership are where a real clinician gets involved: a reviewed Health Snapshot, a personalised Goals & Actions plan, blood work interpretation and prescribing, and direct text access to your doctor.
Does Community Membership include a doctor?+
No — Community Membership is our lightest tier, built for connection and content, not clinical care. If you want a clinician actively involved in your health data, Starter or Longevity Membership are the right fit.
Can I start free and upgrade later?+
Yes. Your free profile and everything you've already logged carries over automatically — upgrading just unlocks the tier you choose on top of your existing profile, nothing is lost or recreated.
What happens after my first 3 months?+
On the one-time plan, your full access ends after 3 months unless you renew. On the subscription, it simply continues into your next quarterly cycle automatically — with a follow-up review built in.
How do I pay?+
Message us on WhatsApp to book your first visit — payment is arranged directly with our clinic team, by bank transfer or in person. Online payment is on its way.
Can I cancel my membership?+
Yes, anytime — for either Community or Longevity Membership, just let us know before your next renewal. Your free profile stays active either way.
