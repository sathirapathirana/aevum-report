Common Questions

Frequently asked

Is the longevity assessment really free? +
Yes — completely. No credit card, no account, no obligation. You complete the form, and your personalised report is delivered to your email automatically within minutes.
Are consultations available online? +
Longevity consultations are currently available online via video call. Contact us via WhatsApp to arrange your appointment.
Do I need to complete the assessment before booking a consultation? +
We recommend it — it gives your doctor a complete picture of your health before the consultation and makes the session significantly more productive. But it is not required.
What is included in the brief skin review with a pack purchase? +
A short consultation with one of our doctors to confirm you have selected the right pack for your skin type and concerns, and to customise the product selection within the pack if needed. It is included free with every skin protocol pack.
How long does a longevity consultation take? +
Initial consultations typically take 45–60 minutes. Follow-up reviews are 20–30 minutes.
